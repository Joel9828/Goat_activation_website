About

Akashic Amit is a rapper and songwriter. He writes hip-hop that leans on honesty more than volume, the kind of songs you put on when you want to think a little clearer.

He started writing to work through his own head, and the music still does that job. There is a point of view in every track, but nothing preachy. Plain writing, a good beat, and enough room for you to find your own meaning in it.

The aim has stayed the same since the start: make something that leaves you a little more awake than you were before it began.

My goal is to help others feel at least 1% more alive each track. Music is either medicine or poison, I choose the first!
