About the Artist

Akashic Amit

Akashic Amit is a storyteller first and a rapper second. He takes the hip-hop he grew up on and uses it to talk about the stuff most people keep quiet: growth, doubt, and the long work of figuring out who you actually are. As he puts it, he doesn't rap for clout, he raps to crack the cage.

The music pulls from real life, old myths, a bit of humor, and lessons learned the hard way. Some tracks make you sit with a thought. Some get you moving. The good ones do both, and none of it pretends to have everything figured out.

"My goal is to help others feel at least 1% more alive each track. Music is either medicine or poison, I choose the first!" Akashic Amit
